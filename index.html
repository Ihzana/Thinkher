Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) Basics

What is CBT?

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is a type of psychotherapy that focuses on identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors.

How CBT Works

CBT works by helping individuals identify and challenge their negative thoughts and behaviors, and replace them with more positive and constructive ones.

CBT Techniques

  • Cognitive restructuring
  • Exposure therapy
  • Mindfulness-based CBT
